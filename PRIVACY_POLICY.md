# Privacy Policy — LinkedIn Jobs Filter

_Last updated: March 2026_

## Overview

LinkedIn Jobs Filter is a browser extension that runs entirely on your device.
It does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data storage

All settings and history are stored locally on your device using the Chrome
`storage.local` API:

| What is stored | Why | Where |
|----------------|-----|--------|
| Filter preferences (hide applied, hide promoted, etc.) | Remember your settings between sessions | Local device only |
| Job IDs you have manually marked as applied | Power the "Hide Applied" feature | Local device only |
| Keyword lists (hide keywords) | Apply your filters automatically | Local device only |

## Data we do NOT collect

- No browsing history
- No personally identifiable information (PII)
- No analytics or telemetry
- No data is ever sent to any server

## Third-party services

This extension makes no network requests of its own. It reads content from
LinkedIn pages you have already loaded in your browser.

## Changes to this policy

Any future updates to this policy will be reflected in the extension version
notes published on the Chrome Web Store.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on the project's
GitHub repository.
