LinkedIn Jobs Filter

Help, support, and local testing notes for the unpacked extension.

What this extension does

  • Hides LinkedIn job cards marked as Applied, Promoted, Viewed, or Dismissed.
  • Lets you dismiss jobs locally with a Dismiss button added to each card.
  • Blocks jobs containing your saved blocked keywords.
  • Can hide jobs older than a chosen number of hours.
  • Can scan the LinkedIn details pane for each currently loaded job and show only cards whose details match your include keywords.
  • Stores settings locally in Chrome using chrome.storage.local.

How to load it locally

  1. Open chrome://extensions.
  2. Enable Developer mode.
  3. Click Load unpacked.
  4. Select the extension folder that contains manifest.json.
  5. Open https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/search.
  6. Pin the extension and use the popup to toggle filters.

How detail-keyword scanning works

  1. Add one or more include keywords in the popup.
  2. Turn on Show only jobs whose details match include keywords.
  3. Click Scan current results.
  4. The extension selects each currently loaded LinkedIn card, waits for the right-side details pane to fully switch to that job, caches the result, and hides only confirmed non-matching jobs after results are available.

Cards that have not been scanned yet stay visible until a scan result exists. Failed scans also stay visible so they are not mistaken for true non-matches.

Keyword precedence

  • Blocked keywords hide matching cards first.
  • The details include-keyword filter then keeps only jobs whose scanned details match your include keywords.
  • Pending or failed detail scans stay visible until you run the scan again successfully.

Testing checklist

  • Confirm the popup opens and settings persist after reloading the extension.
  • Click a job card, then enable Hide Viewed Jobs to confirm local viewed tracking works.
  • Use the injected Dismiss button and confirm the card disappears when Hide Dismissed Jobs is enabled.
  • Enable Hide Promoted Jobs and verify cards containing the Promoted label are removed.
  • Add a blocked keyword such as intern and confirm matching cards disappear.
  • Add include keywords, run Scan current results, and confirm only jobs whose details contain those keywords remain visible.
  • Enable the hours filter and confirm older postings are removed when LinkedIn shows age labels such as 3 hours ago or 2 days ago.
  • Scroll the results list to verify newly loaded cards stay visible until they are scanned.

Known limitations

  • This version targets linkedin.com/jobs/search only.
  • Some filters depend on LinkedIn exposing visible labels in the job card text.
  • The detail-keyword filter depends on LinkedIn rendering the right-side details pane correctly for the selected card.
  • If LinkedIn changes its DOM structure or labels, selectors may need updates.
  • The hours filter only applies when the posting age appears in the card text.

Privacy

No external service is used. Settings, scan cache, viewed history, and dismissed jobs stay local to your browser profile.
